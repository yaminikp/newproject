Careers

The ODL platform seeks to recruit anyone who knows or wishes to learn a skilled trade. ODL provides employment opportunities to unemployed people and also to people who are already working in a trade. It provides training opportunities to unskilled people who want to learn a trade in order to build a better future for themselves, and the opportunity for skilled retirees to give back to their communities by training the new generation. It will take the contributions of all such people to build a better society.
